The Recovery Room is locally owned and operated by Teresa Sinkowski. The business was established in December 1988. It is a custom upholstery shop specializing in residential furniture.

Customer satisfaction is the backbone of our business. The majority of our customers come to us as referrals from previous customers or as repeat business.

We have an extensive library of fabric samples from several reputable suppliers such as Joanne/J.F. Fabrics, Maxwell Fabrics, Barrow Industries, Robert Allen, Westgate, Michael Jon, Exclusive, J. Ennis Fabrics, Carole, Four Seasons, Caravan/Triden, Bert Woll. See the Fabrics page for links to some of our suppliers’ websites.

The Recovery Room has thousands of fabric samples to choose from.
